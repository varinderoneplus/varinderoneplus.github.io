Setup Guide (OBS Studio)

How to Connect to OBS Studio

  • Step 1 ‚Äî Prepare MiniTuber Lite

    This is the most important step to get your avatar appearing over your game or desktop.

    1. Open MiniTuber Lite.

    2. Click the "Reset Green" button in the General tab.

    3. The background will turn a solid bright green.

    Use a bright, uniform green ‚Äî it makes chroma keying in OBS much easier.

  • Step 2 ‚Äî Add to OBS Studio

    1. Open OBS Studio.

    2. In the Sources box click the (+) Plus icon and select Window Capture.

    3. Name it "Avatar" and click OK.

    4. In the Window dropdown select [MiniTuberLite.exe] MiniTuber Lite and click OK.

  • Step 3 ‚Äî Remove the Green Background

    1. Right-click your new "Avatar" source in OBS and choose Filters.

    2. Click the (+) icon at the bottom-left of the Filters window and select Chroma Key, then click OK.

    3. The default settings usually work perfectly ‚Äî you should see the green disappear, leaving only your character.

    4. Click Close. You can now resize and move your avatar anywhere on the screen.

    If the avatar has green edges or flicker, try adjusting Smoothness or Similarity in the Chroma Key filter.

MiniTuber Lite ‚Äî Settings Quick Reference

Press ESC to toggle the Settings Menu on and off.

üè† General Tab

Load BG: Change the background image (useful if you aren't using OBS).

Reset Green: Sets background to Green for Chroma Keying.

Load Prop: Import a PNG or GIF image (e.g., a microphone, a pet, a hat).

Prop Layer: Cycles the prop position:

  • Front: Prop covers the avatar.
  • Middle: Prop is between body and head.
  • Back: Prop is behind the avatar.

Next Skin: Cycles through available avatars.

Next Cam: Switches cameras if you have more than one.

Breath: Toggles the automatic breathing animation.

üòä Face Tab

Laugh Thresh: How wide you must open your mouth to trigger the "Laughing" eyes.

Speak Thresh: How sensitive the mouth movement is when you talk.

üìè Sizes Tab

Char Pos X / Y: Moves the entire character left/right or up/down.

Prop Size: Scales your imported custom image.

BG Scale: Zooms the background image.

üîí Privacy Note

MiniTuber Lite processes all camera data locally on your computer. Your face data is never recorded, stored, or sent to the cloud.

‚Ä¢ Make sure no other app (like Zoom, Teams, or Discord) is currently using your camera.

‚Ä¢ Click the "Next Cam" button to cycle through available video devices.

‚Ä¢ Check your Windows Privacy settings: Go to Settings > Privacy > Camera and ensure "Allow desktop apps to access your camera" is ON.

‚Ä¢ Check your internet connection (required for the first launch verification).

‚Ä¢ Improve your lighting. Webcams track better in well-lit rooms.
