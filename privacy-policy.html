MiniTuber Lite — Privacy & Camera Policy

Last Updated: [Date]

1. Introduction

MiniTuber Lite (we, our, or the Application) is designed to prioritize your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Camera Usage & Face Data

Uses: MiniTuber Lite uses your device's camera solely to track facial movements (head position, blinking, mouth movement) to animate a virtual avatar in real-time.

Local Processing: All face tracking is performed locally on your device using the CPU/GPU.

No Transmission: No images, video feeds, or facial geometry data are sent to external servers, the cloud, or third parties.

No Storage: The Application does not record or store your camera feed or facial data.

3. Data Collection

We do not collect personal information, usage statistics, or identifiers. The Application operates offline (except for optional update checks initiated by the user).

4. Third-Party Services

The Application may provide links to external sites (e.g., Microsoft Store, Support Website). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.
