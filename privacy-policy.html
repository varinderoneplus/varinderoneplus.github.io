Privacy Policy for MiniTuber Lite

Last Updated: 2025-12-13

1. Information We Collect

MiniTuber Lite (we, our, or us) is committed to protecting your privacy. MiniTuber Lite does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

2. Camera and Facial Data

  • Camera Access: The application requires access to your computer's webcam solely for the purpose of real-time face tracking.
  • Facial Data: We process video frames locally on your device to detect facial landmarks (e.g., eye blink, mouth movement, head rotation).
  • No Storage: This video data and the detected facial landmarks are processed in real-time memory (RAM) and are immediately discarded after the avatar is rendered. No video footage or facial data is ever saved to your hard drive or transmitted to the internet.

3. How We Use Information

The data captured by your camera is used exclusively to:

  • Animate the virtual avatar character in real-time.
  • Sync the avatar's movements with your head and facial expressions.

4. Third-Party Data Sharing & Security

Since MiniTuber Lite is a standalone desktop application that processes data locally:

  • We do not share your data with third parties.
  • We do not use analytics software to track your behavior.
  • We do not sell your data.

We prioritize security by ensuring that all processing occurs strictly on your local device. Because no personal data is transmitted over the internet by our application, the risk of data interception is eliminated.
