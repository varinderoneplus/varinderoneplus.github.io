User Manual & Guide

1. Getting Started

Running the App

  • Once installed, Smart Clipboard runs quietly in the background to monitor your clipboard.
  • System Tray: You will see the Smart Clipboard icon (clipboard symbol) in your Windows System Tray (near the clock at the bottom right).
  • Opening the Window: You do not need to click the icon. Simply press the Global Hotkey to open the overlay.

Default Hotkey

Ctrl + Shift + V

Press this combination anywhere on your computer to bring up your clipboard history.

Troubleshooting

Q: The window won't open when I press the hotkey.

A: Check the System Tray (near your clock). If the Smart Clipboard icon isn't there, launch the app from the Start Menu. If it is there, check your Settings to ensure the hotkey hasn't been changed.

Q: Images aren't showing up.

A: The app saves images to a local folder. Ensure you have permission to write files in the installation directory.

Q: How do I stop the app from running?

A: Right-click the Smart Clipboard icon in the System Tray and select "Exit".
